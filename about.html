Our Story

Ridhi Arts Works is an interior design and architecture firm based in Vasai, Maharashtra. Founded by Gaurav Sharma in 2019, the company has quickly gained a reputation for creating beautiful and functional spaces. Some of Ridhi Arts Works' notable projects include the interior design of the Apollo Hospital in Odisha and a range of residential, commercial, and hospitality projects.

The company is known for its attention to detail and commitment to quality, and is dedicated to bringing clients' visions to life. If you're in need of interior design or architecture services, the team at Ridhi Arts Works would be happy to discuss your project and see how they can help.
